# TourGen Presentation - 5 Slides

## Slide 1: Problem Statement & Solution

### Problem Statement:
- Travel booking is fragmented across multiple platforms
- No unified system for tours, hotels, and transport
- Users struggle to compare options easily
- Service providers lack easy market access
- Manual booking processes are time-consuming

### Our Solution - TourGen:
- All-in-one travel booking platform
- Integrated tour, hotel, and transport booking
- Real-time availability and pricing
- Easy provider onboarding
- Automated booking confirmations

---

## Slide 2: Workflow

### User Journey:
1. Register/Login
2. Browse tours/hotels
3. Select package
4. Fill booking details
5. Make payment
6. Get confirmation & invoice

### Provider Journey:
1. Sign up with documents
2. Admin approval
3. Add services (hotels/transport)
4. Manage inventory
5. Receive bookings

---

## Slide 3: Features (Uniqueness)

### For Travelers:
- 50+ tour packages across India
- Hotel search by location/rating/price
- Complete itinerary with day-wise plans
- Compare hotels & transport options
- Book hotel rooms separately
- Download invoices

### For Providers:
- Easy hotel listing with room types
- Transport management with documents
- Real-time inventory tracking
- Booking notifications
- Performance analytics

### For Organizers:
- Create custom tour packages
- Link hotels, transport, guides
- Manage destinations
- Booking analytics with charts

---

## Slide 4: Key Differentiators

### What Makes TourGen Unique:
1. **Comprehensive** - Tours + Hotels + Transport
2. **Multi-stakeholder** - 4 user roles
3. **Scalable** - Modular architecture
4. **Analytics** - Data-driven insights
5. **Approval System** - Quality control
6. **Customizable** - Flexible tour building
